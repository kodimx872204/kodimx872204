### 🏆 Featured Impact Projects (DRC Context)

#### ⚡ High-Performance Data Audit (CNSSAP)
* **The Problem:** Auditing social security contributions for **1.8M+ civil servants** was a manual bottleneck.
* **The Solution:** Built a custom audit engine using **Python & Polars**.
* **Result:** Processed **180MB+ of data in < 2 minutes**, detecting duplicates and anomalies that previously took days to find.

#### 🌍 National Health Budget GIS (Ministry of Health)
* **The Problem:** Invisibility of health expenditure across the 26 provinces of the DRC.
* **The Solution:** Developed a **Django-based GIS platform** to track and map funding in real-time.
* **Result:** Enabled decision-makers to identify funding gaps and rationalize resources across the national territory with interactive map filtering.

#### 📊 Real-Time Bias Detection (Save the Children)
* **The Problem:** Field data collection in rural Kasaï often suffered from collection biases.
* **The Solution:** Engineered **R/Shiny & Python** dashboards connected directly to KoboToolbox.
* **Result:** Provided real-time integrity checks for the "AXE-Filles" program across 9 educational sub-divisions.

#### 🏛️ Social Security Analytics (CNSSAP)
* **The Problem:** Lack of centralized reporting for civil servant social security metrics.
* **The Solution:** Implemented a **Postgres/Python GIS** tool for quarterly reporting and support ratio calculations.
* **Result:** Automated reporting for executives, allowing instant provincial data extraction during high-level meetings.

---

### 📈 Why Work With Me?

In the DRC, "it works on my machine" isn't enough. I build for the **Real World**:
* **Offline-First:** I design systems that collect data without internet and sync seamlessly when back online.
* **Resource Optimized:** My applications are optimized for low-bandwidth environments and entry-level mobile devices.
* **Data Integrity:** I specialize in cleaning and validating "messy" data to ensure leadership makes decisions based on truth.
* **Scalability:** My solutions currently serve **300+ professional users** in critical government and NGO roles.
